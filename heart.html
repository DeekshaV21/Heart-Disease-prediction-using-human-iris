Heart disease is a common health condition affecting millions of people worldwide. It refers to a variety of conditions that affect the heart's structure and function, often leading to serious complications if left untreated. Early detection and proper management are key to reducing the risk of heart disease-related complications.

Importance of Heart Disease

Heart disease is a leading cause of death worldwide, and it's essential to raise awareness about its prevention and early detection. By understanding the risk factors and seeking timely medical attention, many heart disease-related complications can be prevented or managed effectively.

How Heart Diseases Affect Our Iris

While heart diseases themselves don't directly affect the iris, certain health conditions can sometimes manifest in changes to the eyes, including the iris. It's important to monitor your overall health and consult with healthcare professionals if you notice any unusual changes in your eyes or vision.
